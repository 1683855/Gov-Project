Major Issues


  • Jobs

    Browning wishes to bring jobs back to U.S. soil. He vows to abolish minimum wage, in order to prevent labor being exported to foreign countries. Buy American, hire American.

  • Economy

    Browning believes in the power of Reaganomics, in which tax cuts to the corporations will facilitate job growth for all people in the district. Not only will big businesses come back to our district, but all jobs will receive better wages than what they had before.

  • National Security

    After the events of 9/11, Browning swore to prevent any kind of war from reaching U.S. soil. Browning wants to accelerate any military program that gives the U.S. the cutting edge over any enemy, like the F-35 program. Browning also plans to remove any and all restrictions on the use of MOABs and Napalm.

  • Immigration

    Browning firmly believes that immigration is essential to keep American diverse and welcomes all people from the world to immigrate to America to experience the “American Dream.” However, he also believes that in order to keep America safe from foreign predators, an extreme vetting process is necessary. The vetting process requirements include but is not limited to: being able to speak fluent American, being able to name at least 45 American presidents, knowledge in key American ideals, and believing in the one true God, Ronald Reagan. Browning also believes in strengthening the borders to prevent illegal immigration; not only is a wall enough, a dome is necessary to keep out “deplorables.”
