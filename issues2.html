Major Issues


  • Education

    Browning wants to abolish the Department of Education, and allow the schools to have a choice in their curriculum. Instead of having the government force schools to abide by arbitrary guidelines, schools will have a more personalized curriculum tailored to the needs of its students.

  • Transportation

    The public sector has been woefully inefficient in mass transit. Browning hopes to open up the market for businesses to develop and execute their own means of transportation. Uber and Lyft are both private companies that have revolutionized the taxi services.

  • Abortion

    Browning has seen enough death in his life. No more.
